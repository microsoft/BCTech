Prompt
You are teaching controllers how to use the financial reporting feature in Dynamics 365 Business Central.

Based on the articles below (and only those), write up a course called "Learn Business Central financial reporting in 30 days"

The course needs

1. a short introduction on what the financial reporting is and why it is important for controllers

2. a short introduction to the course

3. 30 bite-sized learning lessons: 10 for beginners, 10 intermediate, and 10 for experts.

4. a "References" section to the articles. For each article, summarize it into 3 lines and add a link.

Do not use the term "Account Schedules" as this is the old name for the feature.

The course is aimed at financial controllers who are new to Business Central.

Tone and format: Write in a formal instructional style (like a training manual).

Delivery: The course is supposed to be delivered as a document intended for print or used for a blog post.

## What is a bite-sized learning lesson

A bite-sized learning lesson consists of

1. a short 5-8 line introduction to a topic. Focus on the value proposition (why you need to learn this), rather than the "what"

2. Then an exercise. Exercises most be concrete

3. And then a "Learn more" section with links to relevant articles.

A student should be able to complete a lesson in less than 10 minutes.
