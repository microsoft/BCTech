# 🚀 Stage 1 — Introduction & Setup

> Set up your Business Central environment and verify you're ready to build.

**Stage 1 of 10** · [Overview](../README.md) · Next → [Stage 2: Your First Agent](./02-first-agent.md)

---

## Prerequisites

You'll need a Business Central Sandbox environment with Copilot & AI capabilities enabled. If you don't have one, sign up for a free trial at [businesscentral.dynamics.com](https://businesscentral.dynamics.com).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **What you need**
> - A Business Central Sandbox environment (cloud)
> - A user account with the **SUPER** permission set or equivalent admin rights
> - **Copilot & AI capabilities** enabled in your environment

---

### Task 1 — Sign In

1. Open a browser and go to **[businesscentral.dynamics.com](https://businesscentral.dynamics.com)**
2. Sign in with your credentials
3. You should land on the Business Central home page (Role Centre)

> [!TIP]
> **You're ready when…** you can see the Business Central Role Centre and your company name appears in the top navigation bar.

---

### Task 2 — Enable Copilot & AI Capabilities

If Copilot features are not already active in your environment:

1. Search for **Copilot & AI Capabilities** using the search bar (<kbd>Alt</kbd>+<kbd>Q</kbd>)
2. On the **Copilot & AI Capabilities** page, ensure the relevant capabilities are turned **On**
3. Accept any terms if prompted

---

<details>
<summary>🛟 <strong>Stuck?</strong></summary>

- Make sure the URL includes your environment name — it should be `businesscentral.dynamics.com/<environmentname>`, not just the base URL
- Try an **InPrivate / Incognito** window if your browser auto-fills a different account
- If Copilot options are missing, check that your licence includes Copilot features and that your environment is on a recent version

</details>

---

## ✅ Stage complete when…

- [ ] You're signed into your Business Central sandbox
- [ ] Copilot & AI Capabilities are enabled
